Cosmo The Angry Fish

A quick note about Cosmo

Cosmo is a red Beta Fish which is a Chinese Fighting Fish, and he is constantly angry. His brow is furrowed and he likes to jump up and try to attack whoever comes near.

Here are some of Cosmo's favorite things!

  • Swimming through his castle
  • Frowning
  • Jumping
  • Sleeping on his leaf

Here is an ordered list of some of Cosmo's least favorite things!

  1. Strangers
  2. Having his bowl cleaned
  3. When I buy the wrong brand of fish food
